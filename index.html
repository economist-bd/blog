Welcome to My Blog

Hello and welcome! I'm Monjurul Haque, an economics lecturer passionate about making complex economic theories accessible to everyone. Here, I share my thoughts on current events, breakdowns of economic principles, and updates on my latest research.

My goal is to foster a better understanding of the economic forces that shape our world. Whether you're a student, a fellow academic, or just a curious mind, I hope you find the content here insightful and engaging.

Use the navigation bar below to explore my articles, learn more about my background, or get in touch.

About Me

I hold a Master's degree in Economics from the University of Dhaka and have been teaching at the collegiate level for over five years. My academic interests lie at the intersection of microeconomics, behavioral economics, and public policy.

I believe that economics is not just about graphs and equations; it's a powerful tool for understanding human behavior and solving real-world problems. When I'm not teaching or writing, I enjoy reading historical non-fiction and exploring the local food scene.
